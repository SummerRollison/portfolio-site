Let's Cook

View Live Site
React, JavaScript

Using a REST API, Let's Cook allows you to search for recipes based on any ingredient you have already. Users also have the quick search option when they are just looking for inspiration. Since food allergies are a common concern when meal planning, all food allergens are clearly labeled before the user even clicks on the recipe link.
